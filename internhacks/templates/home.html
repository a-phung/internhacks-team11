Responsibly Examine Data with FairView

FairView is a project run by a group of enthusiastic college students offering a tool to help ML Practitioners and Data Researchers to gain deeper insights into their demographic metadata and realize possible bias/unfairness.

WE BEGAN WITH THE QUESTION:

Why does underlying bias in metadata matter?

Especially in Machine Learning, the problem mainly doesn’t lie in the accuracy of the algorithms but rather in the quality of the training data that developers take in to develop the system. In fact, the data these algorithms rely on reflect underlying biases around race, gender, age, and other factors. When trained on biased data, ML algorithms reflect and perpetuate these biases. “Garbage in, Garbage out”.

2. Translate user needs into data needs

3. Source your data responsibly

4. Prepare and document your data

5. Design for labelers and labeling

6. Tune your model

Examine Your Metadata

With our tool, you would be able to see the distribution of your metadata’s available demographic attributes, for example age, race, education, income, etc. We would also help you uncover several common issues such as the most and least dominant feature values, missing values for a large number of observations.
